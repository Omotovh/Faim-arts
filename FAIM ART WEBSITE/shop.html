Frequently asked questions

If you're paying internationally, I accept payments in up to 53 currencies in the amount equivalent to the payable sum through Flutterwave. Simply go to the contact page and click the "pay here button". Local payments can be made to my bank account or via Flutterwave on the contact page.

There is no fixed price for commissioned work. The amount I charge depends on a combination of factors like paper size, complexity of drawing the piece and the urgency. Contact me through the contact page for further discussions and I'll help you determine a price.

Once you pay for an artpiece, the piece will be packaged and sent over to address you provide me via mail or chat irrespective of your country of residence.

All things being equal, your art piece will arrive at your location within 5 working days from when you make a purchase or a commission is completed and fully paid for.
