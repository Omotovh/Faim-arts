ABOUT THE ART PIECE

This drawing of Gal Gadot as Wonder woman is a personal favourite. I happen to be a huge fan of Gadot and the Wonder woman franchise, and although many people have much to complain about from Wonder woman 1984, i find reasons to love as any fan would. This piece was created using graphite and charcoal pencils through a span of 37hrs and 21 mins.
I am selling the piece via auction and the art work will go ultimately to the highest bidder. Not just because that's the highest sum of money but because that is the person who values it the most.

My minimum selling price is $150 so bidding price starts from that.

Once the piece has been auctioned, the buyer will be contact personally and transportation details will be finalized.

Make your bids and counterbids in the comment section.
